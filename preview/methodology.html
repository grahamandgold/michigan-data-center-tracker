Our one rule

The Live Map is our single source of truth. Every number on every page matches the map. When new sourced information arrives, we update the map first — then everything else follows. We never publish a figure we can't point to a source for.

Our standards
  • We represent supporters and opponents fairly, and we label who's making each claim.
  • We date everything that moves — these figures change weekly.
  • When a number is disputed, we say so instead of picking a side.
  • Nothing here is legal or investment advice.
See a mistake?

Corrections make the data better. If a figure looks off, a project moved, or a pause was lifted, tell us and we'll check the record.
