Method

Each dot is a Michigan community that adopted a formal data-center moratorium, pause, or ban, placed at the community and dated to the month the measure passed. Built from municipal ordinances and meeting minutes; a community is counted once, at its first pause.

Method

Pressure score = active projects + adopted local pauses + hearings scheduled in the next 30 days, summed per community. It is an editorial index for triage — a relative signal of where to look, not an official measurement. Dot size and color scale with the score.

Method

Cumulative disclosed nameplate load (MW) across tracked projects, plotted by the month each figure entered the public record.
