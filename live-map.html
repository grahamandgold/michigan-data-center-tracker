Method · how we group regions▾

Records are bucketed into four regions by their county of record — a fixed lookup, not a guess. The buckets follow how Michiganders and state agencies commonly talk about the state:

SE Michigan
Wayne, Oakland, Macomb, Washtenaw, Monroe, Livingston, St. Clair, Lenawee.
Mid-Michigan
The I-69/I-96 corridor — Ingham, Clinton, Eaton, Jackson, Genesee, Saginaw, Bay, Midland and neighbors.
West Michigan
The lakeshore & southwest — Kent, Ottawa, Kalamazoo, Calhoun, Berrien, Van Buren, Muskegon and neighbors.
Northern Michigan
Everything above the line, incl. the northern Lower Peninsula and the entire U.P.

Regions are an editorial convenience for navigation — they are not official state planning districts.
